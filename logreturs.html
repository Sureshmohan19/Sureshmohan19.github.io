Simple Returns

In finance, simple return of an asset (or an investment) can be calculated using the following mathematical formula,

r t = p t - p t - 1 p t - 1

Obviously, everyone will be familiar with this calculation. The method will also helps calculating the investors' wealth over a period of time in an investment.
